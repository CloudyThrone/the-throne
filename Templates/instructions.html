How to Register with M-Pesa

Follow these steps to register your account:

  1. Open your M-Pesa menu.
  2. Select Lipa na M-Pesa.
  3. Choose Buy Goods and Services.
  4. Enter the Till Number: 6959497.
  5. Enter the amount: KES 100.
  6. Complete the transaction and save the confirmation message.

After a successful payment, please share the M-Pesa confirmation message and your username
with the company's email at skyqash@gmail.com, our able mentors,
or on any of our official platforms in await for your account to be approved and also for faster approval, Thank you.

In case of anything, contact us at skyqash@gmail.com.
